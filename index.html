About Me

I am a Ph.D. student in computer engineering at Sharif University of Technology. You can find more about me on my CV.
My research focuses on developing machine learning models and algorithms for probabilistic analysis of social networks dynamics. It has practical applications in predicting users' behaviors, control and shaping of users' activities, and providing context-aware recommendations.
By the way, I am Ali Zarezade. Contact me at: familyname(at)ce.sharif.edu
